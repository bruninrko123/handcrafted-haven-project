## Overview
**Handcrafted Haven** is a full-stack web application designed to showcase and sell unique handcrafted items. It serves as a digital marketplace that connects artisans with customers who value creativity, craftsmanship, and sustainable consumption.

The platform empowers creators to share their stories and products while providing users with an intuitive, accessible, and secure browsing experience. By fostering community engagement and supporting local artisans, Handcrafted Haven aims to redefine how handmade goods are discovered and appreciated online.

## Features

### Seller Profiles
- Authenticated sellers have dedicated profiles
- Artisans can showcase their craftsmanship and personal stories
- Sellers can add, update, and manage product descriptions

### Product Listings
- Artisans can list handcrafted items with:
  - Descriptions
  - Pricing
  - Images
- Users can browse the catalog and filter products by:
  - Category
  - Price range
  - Other relevant criteria

### Reviews & Ratings
- Any user can leave:
  - A star rating
  - A written review for products
